### Drag optimization in BAJA vehicle (July 2016 - November 2016)

The objective of this project was to analyze the flow around an BAJA vehicle, fabricated following the BAJA SAE rule book. Optimizations are made to reduce the drag created due to firewall and body panels. 
Main concern of this project was to reduce the power loss due to drag, which reduces the engine power available. Also, by such optimization, fuel economy is the vehicle is improved, which in turn improves on the endurance of the vehicle and Vehicle speed.
Engine used in such BAJA vehicle is a 10hp, 305cc engine. Drag force produced, decreases the engine power by 1.8hp, which reduces the engine efficiency. 
Optimizations made:
1) Rear end vortex is reduced by adding roof and rear cover.
2) Wake turbulence behind the vehicle is reduced by adding diffuser and convert body panels. 
3) Nose panel is added to divert the flow over the firewall.
4) Under body is made linear and covered 

Such optimization results in a reduced drag coefficient from 0.89 to 0.71, with increased down force and increased engine efficiency.

### Structural Design and Optimization of Automotive Chassis (September 2015 - April 2016)

Space frame chassis designed for BAJA SAE Off-Road vehicle application.
Objective of Chassis design is Endurance and sustainability, and optimizations are made for Reduced Weight while satisfying performance requirements and BAJA SAE rules.
The design of chassis is focused on improving Bending stiffness, Torsional stiffness and crashworthiness in case of Head-on and rear impacts.
